My Story

I began my journey as a curious 15-year-old, eager to make a mark in the digital world. I started with trading—late nights, countless videos, books—but eventually realized the capital required was too much to sustain.

At 16, I discovered the agency model and launched Roomreach. I reached out to clients but got no responses. It was tough, but I didn’t give up. I then tried starting a clothing brand—it didn’t take off. I launched another agency, Redx, but again, no luck with clients.

Next was dropshipping. I ran into issues with Shopify and had to pause. But each setback taught me something new.

Now at 17, I’ve found a passion for digital products—and that’s how Cryio was born. I’m still learning, still building, and far from where I want to be. But one thing’s for sure—I’m not stopping anytime soon.
